Petrol Pal

Summary

Petrol Pal was the project my group worked on in COM S 309 at Iowa State University. The idea behind this app was to create a "Social Media for Gas Stations". General consumers could leave reviews of stations and find the price of gas at them. They can also favorite stations and contact them with questions or to ask for assistance. On the gas station side of things, stations could update their prices, approve updates from general public people that reported price descrepancies, reply to direct messages from their customers and provide a deal of the day.

In this project, I was a Front-End developer along with my friend and classmate Noah Ross. Together we learned the process of app development for Android.

For my work on this project, I was awarded one of the Best Coder awards.
